About us

Agency represents a pool of professional photographers who are carefully selected for each assignment, depending on their particular field of expertise and the nature of the photo shoot.

From institutional and industrial photos, to corporate branding and commercials, our dedicated team of professionals, who have many years of photography between them, provide Agency with the experience, set to meet the needs of any corporate photography assignment in Sweden.

With any kind of requirements from our clients, the flexibility and skills of our photographers allow us to satisfy your needs.
